Poems on sustainable AI at my blog "The AI has gone bananas"

I consider myself a statistically rare mix of science and literature constantly trying to embrace my juxtapositions. Recently this has crystallised in a blog named The AI has gone bananas where I write free verse poems (such as "The future of AI is female") at the intersection of artificial intelligence, social issues and environmental sustainability.
