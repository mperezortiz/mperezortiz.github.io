Who am I?

I am a researcher in artificial intelligence at UCL, interested in the societal and environmental impact of technology. At the moment my work is divided between generalisation analysis for neural networks, recommender systems and user modelling in education settings, human-centered AI and AI for sustainable development. For more info on this check my latest publications in my Google Scholar.
